Motoristas

Dial a car always welcomes enthusiastic, reliable, have a good knowledge of London places and speak fluent and professional English, to apply and become our company driver.

You will need a ‘Transport for London Private Hire License’ (formely known as a PCO license) in order to work with us.

The more journeys you complete, the more money you will receive.

All drivers are Self-Employed. It is the driver’s responsibility to ensure that his vehicle always contains enough fuel to complete each journey.

All vehicles have satellite navigation with PDA(s). Furthermore, all drivers will be required backgrounds checks. Before starting work with us, you will need to provide us following documents:

• Driving license (both parts)

• PCO license (both parts)

• Two proves of identification clearly showing your current address

• National Insurance No. (NI)

• Motor Ordinance Tax (MOT)

• Vehicle insurance

• CRB certificate

• One personal latest photograph

You must have a smart saloon, estate, 7-seater or Executive car and be ready to join one of London’s fastest growing cab company. We offer excellent rates of pay. Please call Dial A Car and become a part.
