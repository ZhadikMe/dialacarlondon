About Us

Dial a Car offer service 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, we are providing on one call cabs service to transport needs whether you’re roaming surrounded by London or further afield.

Dial a Car have been providing service in London since 1989. We are hiring only licensed driver and minicab operators, so customers can have the benefit of a very secure and happy journey.

We have a varied fleet of cabs including of Saloons (sedan, taxi), Estates (Station Wagon), MPVs (Passenger Minivans), 8 seater, and executive car. Business clients with usual require for on call mini cab service and London airport transfer be able to benefit from our credit account amenities.

We cover all foremost airports like Heathrow Airport, Luton Airport, Stansted Airport, Gatwick Airport, and London City Airport.
